Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the FlashCard App ("the App") handles user information. The App is designed as an offline learning tool and does not collect personal information from users.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics

The App works primarily offline on your device.

3. Local Data Storage

The App stores learning progress locally on your device only. This may include:

  • Flashcard reading counts
  • Starred (favorite) terms
  • Learning progress statistics
  • Last viewed card or category

This data is stored in the app's local database and is not transmitted to any external servers.

4. Internet Usage

The App does not require an internet connection and does not send data to remote servers.

5. Data Sharing

Because the App does not collect personal data, no information is shared with third parties.

6. Data Security

All app data is stored locally within your device’s application storage. No external transmission of data occurs.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, the App is safe for general educational use.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any updates will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
